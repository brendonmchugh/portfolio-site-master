Clarendon URW

Clarendon URW was an assignment to create a total of eight pages. In this project the point was to give the history of the font. For the first piece I ended up using one of my other projects I did with the same font, that was the cover pages. After that I then added a theme to each of the pages but I followed a similar layout for the text throughout the whole book. On the third page I made it a brithsh themed page because it went with the history of the font. For the next few slides I wanted to show different examples about the text in different contexts. On the last page before the back cover I put a wanted poster of the creater of Clarendon

John Holladay

John Holladay was a biography of his artwork. John Holladay used to be my first graphic design teacher so I chose to make this project about him. The project was to pick a know artist and put their work and a little information about them into a book. The book is mainly made up of Holladays most accomplished and recognized work out there.
