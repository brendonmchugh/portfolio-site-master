LJ Shoe

LJ Shoe was a vector drawing/tracing of my long jump shoe for track. This was a class assignment where we had to choose an item that had a special meaning to us and I chose these shoes because they remind me of the thing I love to do which is track. In this project the goal was to trace in item using the pen tool as best you can and try to capture what the item does with minimal words. Because these are Long Jump soes they are meant to help you in long jump which means helping you just slightly higher while also going farther. These shoes are also one of the best shoes out currently which is why I put "JUMP SMARTER" since it the best option for any jumper. Then in the bottom right corner I put the name of the shoes and the year they were made.
